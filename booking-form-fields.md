# Cruise Booking Inquiry — Google Form Field List

Build this in Google Forms, then embed it on the "Book a Cruise" page (see instructions in that file).

**Form title:** Cruise Booking Inquiry — Poverty to Paradise Travel
**Form description:** Tell me a bit about the trip you're dreaming of and I'll send back real options within a few days. No obligation to book.

---

1. **Full name** — Short answer, required

2. **Email address** — Short answer, required
   - Enable response validation: must be a valid email

3. **Phone number** — Short answer, required

4. **Are you booking for yourself, a group, or asking about an upcoming group trip?**
   Multiple choice, required
   - Just me / my travel companion(s)
   - A group I'm organizing
   - I want to join one of your group trips

5. **Preferred cruise line(s)** — Checkboxes, optional
   - Princess
   - Carnival
   - Royal Caribbean
   - Virgin Voyages
   - Holland America
   - Celebrity
   - Not sure / open to recommendations
   - Other: ___

6. **Where do you want to go?** — Short answer, optional
   (e.g. Caribbean, Alaska, Mediterranean, "wherever's cheapest")

7. **Travel dates** — Short answer, required
   Helper text: "Exact dates, a rough month, or 'flexible' is fine."

8. **How many travelers?** — Short answer, required

9. **Cabin type preference** — Multiple choice, optional
   - Interior (best value)
   - Ocean view
   - Balcony
   - Suite
   - Not sure — show me options

10. **Budget range per person** — Multiple choice, required
    - Under $500
    - $500–$1,000
    - $1,000–$2,000
    - $2,000+
    - Not sure yet

11. **Any special occasion?** — Short answer, optional
    (birthday, anniversary, retirement trip, etc.)

12. **Anything else I should know?** — Paragraph, optional

---

### Settings to turn on in Google Forms
- **Settings → Responses → Collect email addresses** (so you have it even if they mistype the field)
- **Settings → Responses → Get email notifications for new responses**
- Link responses to a **Google Sheet** (Responses tab → green Sheets icon) so Muhammad or you can track/follow up systematically, same as your partnership tracker
