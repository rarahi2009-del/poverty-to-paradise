Why go as a group

Same paradise. Better company.

Group departures come with perks solo bookings don't — better pricing, onboard meetups, and a built-in circle of people who already get why you're doing this. You still book your own cabin, your own budget, your own way. You just don't sail in alone.

Group Pricing

Better rates, real savings

Group bookings often unlock pricing and perks that aren't available booking solo — savings Lynnelle passes straight to you.

Built-in Community

Meet the crew onboard

A casual meetup once you're sailing — no obligation, just a friendly face if you want one. Many P2P travelers have become lifelong friends.

Still Your Trip

Your cabin, your pace

Group trip doesn't mean group itinerary. Come to everything or none of it — it's still your vacation, your way.
