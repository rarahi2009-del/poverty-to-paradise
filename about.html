The story so far

I sold the house, left the job, and found paradise for $60 a day.

Hi, I'm Lynnelle. A few years ago I walked away from a life that was draining me — and decided the version of retirement I'd been sold wasn't the only option.

So I sold the house, sold the car, and moved onto a cruise ship. Not for a week. For good. What I found was that living full-time at sea, done the smart way, can cost less than staying on land — around $2,000 to $2,500 a month, all in. Room. Food. Entertainment. Everything.

I started sharing how I make it work on YouTube as Poverty to Paradise, and it turns out I wasn't the only one who needed to hear that this was possible. Now, alongside the channel, I'm a licensed travel agent — which means I can take everything I've learned about getting real value out of a cruise and use it to book your trip, at no extra cost to you.

Whether you're dreaming of one incredible cruise or wondering if a life like mine is possible for you too, I'd love to help you get there.
