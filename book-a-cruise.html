A few things people ask

Does it cost more to book through you?

No. The cruise line pays the agent commission — your fare is the same as booking direct, but now you've got someone in your corner.

How do I actually pay?

Once you've chosen a cruise, I'll send a secure payment link to complete your booking. No card details go through the form itself.

What if I'm not sure on dates yet?

That's fine — note that you're flexible on the form and I'll bring you a few windows that make sense for your budget and destination.

Which cruise lines do you work with?

All the majors — Princess, Carnival, Royal Caribbean, Virgin Voyages, Holland America, Celebrity, and more. If it sails, I can likely book it.
