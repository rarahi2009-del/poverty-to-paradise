Frequently Asked Questions

Does it cost more to book through you?

No. The cruise line pays the agent commission — your fare is the same as booking direct, but now you've got someone in your corner.

Does it cost extra to use this service?

No. The cruise line pays the travel advisor's commission, not you. Your cruise fare is the same as booking directly with the cruise line, but you'll also have someone to help you before, during, and after the booking process.

How do I pay for my cruise?

Once you've selected your cruise, you'll receive a secure payment link to complete your reservation. Your payment information is never entered through this inquiry form.

What if I haven't decided on a cruise yet?

No problem. Let us know what you're looking for, and we'll help you narrow down the best ships, itineraries, and sailing dates for your travel style and budget.

Can you book any cruise line?

Yes. Whether you already know exactly which cruise you want or you're still deciding, we can help you book virtually all major cruise lines.
